Research Positions

Research positions for PhD and Postdoc are available in our group in Institute of Applied Physics and Materials Engineering (IAPME) at the University of Macau (UM). Our research focuses on rational design and synthesis of smart materials, fabrication and applications development of energy conversion and storage device.

Ph.D. Scholarship

We have opened positions for highly motivated PhD students. If you study materials science, physics, and chemistry, or come from engineering-backgrounds such as electronic/chemical/mechanical engineering, and are interested in our research, please send me a CV and cover letter.

Qualifications for all the PhD students (Chinese version: Link)

- Bachelor or master students from reputable schools (e.g., top 200 universities of any recognized world university rankings or Project 985/211 universities in Mainland China are preferable).

- English Proficiency Requirement: Obtained a TOEFL* score of 550 (paper-based examination) / 80 (internet-based examination) or, Obtained an IELTS* score of 6.0 or above with no sub-score lower than 5.5 or, Obtained a CET6 score of 430 or above.

PhD scholarship catalog

1). UM Macao PhD. scholarship: A monthly stipend of MOP20,000 and a conference and research related travel allowance of MOP10,000 per academic year for a period up to four years.

Postdoctoral Researcher and others

1). UM Macao Postdoc Fellowship: Monthly remuneration of MOP40,000. Annual conference and research related trips allowance of MOP20,000. The candidates should possess outstanding research achievements (Top-journals, patents, etc), and graduated in the past 2 years. Link

Deadline: 1st batch: on or before 31/05; 2nd batch: on or before 30/09, every year

2). Normal Postdoc Researcher: We will offer the competitive salary for the applicants, and the salary is normally supported by the group research funding. No opening position at this moment.

3). Other positions: Not open position for Research Assistant, Research Associate at this moment, and it will be updated from time to time.
