Biographical Information

Dr. Tang obtained his BS degree in applied chemistry and MS degree in materials science at the Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2006 and 2008, respectively, under the guidance of Prof Tao Jie. He graduated from Nanyang Technological University (NTU, Singapore) with a PhD in Materials science (2013), developing functional materials for environmental protection under the guidance of Prof Dong Zhili and Prof Chen Zhong. After that, he joined Prof Chen Xiaodong’ group as a postdoctoral fellow at the NTU, working on advanced functional materials for energy conversion and storage application. In Sep 2018, he joins Institute of Applied Physics and Materials Engineering (IAPME) at the University of Macau as an Assistant Professor. So far, He has published over 70 scientific articles with 25 papers as first and corresponding authors including Chem. Soc. Rev., Adv. Mater., Angew. Chem., Adv. Energy Mater., Adv. Funct. Mater., Small, etc.
